Background

Mark Faatz is a licensed Realtor at Nebraska Realty, he is a close friend of mine. Mark grew up on a farm. On that farm, he learned a lot about life, particularly the value of hard work. After graduating from college with a Bachelor of Science in finance and marketing, Mark began his professional career in corporate America as an executive with Fortune 500 companies such as Kelloggs, ConAgra Foods, and Boden Dairy. In this position, he led teams of professional sales results due to strategic thinking and planning

How Mark became a realator

Mark was returning home from Israel when Covid hit in March 2020. Thats when I said, Maybe I should apply myself in real estate and see what I can do, he recalled. Even in our hours, sometimes the greatest tragedies have a way of turning into something positive. Little did Mark know that his journey into real estate would open doors to some of the happiest times of his life. He spent two months writing a 36-month strategic plan.
On June 1, 2020, Mark unofficially kicked off his real estate career at Wood Bros Realty and experienced great success early on. He was ranked number four in August 2020 and number three in the company in June of 2021 and had moved up to a spot to number two by July. After being an executive in corporate America, I enjoy wrapping my head around my clients, whether theyre buying or selling, Mark said. Its important to me to focus on what is important to my customer and deliver meaningful value.

As every eal estate professional knows, you are not merely a salespeson; Realtors wear many different hats. Sometimes, you are your clients rock and sounding board. you are mediators, negotiators, and bearers of good- and sometimes, bad - news.
